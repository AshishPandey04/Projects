Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. First rinse 650 grams carrots (8 to 9 medium-sized tender, juicy carrots or 6 to 7 long carrots) a few times in water.Drain the water. Peel the carrots using a vegetable peeler. Set the peeled carrots aside.
  2. Ensure that the carrots are juicy and tender. You could use any variety of carrots (red or orange), but they should be tender.
  3. Next, grate the carrots with a box grater or in a food processor. You can measure the grated carrots with a standard measuring cup and you will need about 4 to 4.5 cups of grated carrots.
  4. In a heavy kadai or wok, add all of the grated carrots.Pour in 4 cups full-fat milk. Switch on the burner.Mix the grated carrots and milk together.
  5. Cook this mixture on a low-medium heat. After a few minutes, the milk will begin to froth and then start slowly reducing.
  6. Keep stirring this halwa mixture often, scraping the sides of the kadai to remove the evaporated milk solids. Stir the milk solids into the cooking mixture.
  7. Cook the carrot and milk mixture until the liquid is reduced by 75%........

Recipe
